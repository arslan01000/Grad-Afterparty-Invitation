Chapter I  ·  The Beginning

It started innocently enough. A group of students walked into North American University when Bitcoin was still cheap, Game of Thrones was still good, and the future felt wide open.

Some chose CS & Software Engineering — the degree everyone swore was untouchable. Others chose Business — convinced the corner office was just four years away. A fearless few chose Education & Psychology — to heal the world, while quietly needing healing themselves.

"None of them had any idea what was coming."

They had deadlines, group projects with people who never replied, and a deep, unshakeable belief that it would all make sense eventually.
